About our Restaurant

The Restaurant

AMA Restaurant promises to deliver a satisfying experience full of warmth and hospitality, serving creative Arabian cuisine, building and maintaining high standard relationships with its guests.

Situated in an exclusive part of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, AMA offers the most exciting and luxurious fine dining experience in the Middle East and beyond. With such a variety we are sure to please every guest. At AMA you will enjoy a unique Middle Eastern culinary from Syrian Hummus to Saudi Kabsa.

  • Our Experience

  • The Founders Meet

    The founders of AMA Ahmad, Mustafa, and ahmed met at the University of Prince Sultan. They all shared a common passion which was to bring joy upon the faces of their food testers, to craft recipes that were extraordinary, and most importantly to unleash their entrepenuership.

  • Create A Restaurant

    They decided to open their very first branch in Beirut, Lebanon.

  • Home to 100+ employees

    It has employed over 100 employees and counting.


  • More Restaurants Outlet

  • Stablished Restaurant in different parts of the globe.

    AMA has opened in 3 continents: Asia (Middle East), Europe, and North America.

  • Franchised Restaurant

    After opening the first branch, AMA has been admired and loved by the people and is currently being franchised in 2 new locations: Riyadh and New York.

  • And for another 100 to come

    We are looking to expand the AMA family with hosting more and more employees in future branches.

  • Future Plans

    AMA is always looking to improve and better it services in an immaculate fashion. AMA is looking to expand its menu, grow in employess, and most importantly expand to more and more parts of the wold in more continents.
