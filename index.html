The Best Arabian Cuisine in KSA

The Restaurant

AMA Restaurant promises to deliver a satisfying experience full of warmth and hospitality, serving creative Arabian cuisine, building and maintaining high standard relationships with its guests.

Situated in an exclusive part of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, AMA offers the most exciting and luxurious fine dining experience in the Middle East and beyond. With such a variety we are sure to please every guest. At AMA you will enjoy a unique Middle Eastern culinary from Syrian Hummus to Saudi Kabsa.
